Our Vission

.

IEEE PES IIUC Student Branch Chapter is one of the technical society chapter of IEEE IIUC Student Branch, which itselft is one of the student run organization under the supervision of IEEE Bangladesh Section. IEEE PES IIUC SBC has started its journey on 23 Feb 2021. The Power & Energy Society (PES) provides the world's largest forum for sharing the latest in technological developments in the electric power industry, for developing standards that guide the development and construction of equipment and systems, and for educating members of the industry and the general public. We are dedicated to provide scientific and engineering information on electric power and energy for the betterment of society. We nurture the youngsters to be the next-generation leaders in power sector. Thus we are committed to provide professional skill development training to our members. On April 2021, we celebrated the IEEE PES Day'21 throughout the month in our student branch. We are all set to organize year-the-round state of art technical and education activities.

Our Goal
IEEE PES IIUC Student Branch Chapter focused on the scientific & engineering knowledge about electric power and energy for the betterment of the world. It got its approval on February 18, 2021. Our mission is to develop standards and empower the development of technology software and best practices in all area of electric power and energy. We offers various networking opportunities for our members and also allows to access to peer reviewed technical Journals and educational materials with a view to remain up to date.
